Who we are

Our Story

Born from a love of slow mornings and great coffee, Krafe was built for the people of Cambodia.

How it started

A cozy space for coffee, work, and good conversation.

Krafe Coffee is built for people who love calm design, quality coffee, and a warm place to meet. Every drink is prepared with care, from the first grind to the final pour.

We source 100% Arabica beans from small farms and roast them fresh each week. Our bakers arrive before dawn so every pastry is warm when you walk in.

What drives us

Our values

Three simple commitments we make to every guest, every day.

Quality first

We never cut corners. Every bean is selected, every drink timed, every pastry baked fresh. If it isn't good enough for us, it doesn't reach you.

Community

Krafe is more than a café — it's a space where people gather, ideas form, and friendships grow. We're proud to be part of Phnom Penh's daily life.

Sustainability

We partner with ethical farms, minimise packaging waste, and compost our coffee grounds. Caring for the planet is part of every cup we serve.
